Who We Are

We are a robotics team consisting of six people in middle school and high school. Our youngest member is in 7th grade and our oldest is in 9th. Our team was created in the Fall of 2022, bringing together variety of people who all shared one characteristic; the desire to learn. Since then, our team has put in countless hours of hard work to make our current achievements possible.

2022-2023 Season Achievements

In the qualification rounds, we placed second. We were the alliance captain of our own alliiance.
