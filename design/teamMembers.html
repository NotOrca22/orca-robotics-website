Sophia Wang

Co-Captain, Main CAD Designer

Sophia is 13 years old and in 8th grader. She is the co-captain who founded this team alongside her brother Christopher. Sophia is also the main CAD designer in Orca Robotics. She uses Onshape to design parts to print out with the 3-D printer. Although she is new to robotics, she and the team aim to do their best and hopefully succeed.
